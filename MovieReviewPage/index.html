Maze Runner

The Maze Runner is a young adult dystopian science fiction novel (and film) featuring Thomas, a teenager who arrives in "the Glade" with no memory of his past. He joins a community of boys trapped by a massive, ever-changing maze patrolled by deadly, biomechanical "Grievers," forcing them to find an exit.
