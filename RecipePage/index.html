Ice Candy

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making refreshing ice candy! These colorful frozen treats are perfect for cooling down on a hot day without spending too much. Follow this simple recipe to create delicious, sweet, and icy ice candy that’s bursting with flavor and fun in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups Water
  • 1/2 cup Cornstarch (diluted in water to avoid lumps)
  • 1/2 cup White sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 can (410ml) Evaporated milk
  • 1 can (395g) Condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup Powdered milk (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • Ice candy plastic bags (1.5" x 10" or 2" x 10")
  • Small funnel

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine water, sugar, and cornstarch. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and becomes translucent.
  2. Turn off the heat. Mix in the evaporated milk, condensed milk, and powdered milk until smooth.
  3. Allow the mixture to cool down completely.
  4. Add ingredients like shredded cheese, fruit, or cocoa.
  5. Using a funnel, fill each plastic bag about 3/4 full.
  6. Tie the top of the plastic bags securely and freeze until solid.
